Medical and regulatory design

Safe medicine access comes first

Rwanda E-Pharmacy is designed around licensed pharmacy participation, prescription control, responsible professional oversight, private health data, and pharmacy verification.

  • Verified pharmacies before public listing
  • Prescription-only medicines require pharmacist confirmation
  • Stock freshness score and last-updated timestamps
  • Private prescription storage and signed access links
  • Aggregated analytics with patient identity removed

Launch compliance checklist

Regulatory review, privacy policy, pharmacy SOP, delivery SOP, data sharing agreements, and incident response plan.
