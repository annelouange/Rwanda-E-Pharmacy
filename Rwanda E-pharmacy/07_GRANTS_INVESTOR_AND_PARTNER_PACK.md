# Grants, Investor, and Partner Pack

## Funding Narrative

Rwanda E-Pharmacy improves access to essential medicines by connecting patients with verified pharmacy stock, insurance support, and delivery options while creating privacy-safe medication data for health planning.

It supports universal health coverage, chronic disease continuity, medicine availability, pharmacy digitization, and evidence-based public health decision-making.

## Grant Themes

- Universal health coverage.
- Digital health infrastructure.
- Essential medicine access.
- Rural and provincial equity.
- Chronic disease care.
- Pharmacy supply chain strengthening.
- Health data for policy.
- Youth innovation and employment.
- Patient safety and regulated delivery.

## Funder-Ready Deliverables

- Pilot platform.
- Verified pharmacy network.
- Medicine availability dashboard.
- Chronic medicine refill workflow.
- Patient access report.
- Pharmacy stock quality report.
- Stock-out and price variation analytics.
- Regulatory and data governance framework.

## Investor Thesis

The platform starts with a high-frequency patient pain point and expands into pharmacy SaaS, delivery, institutional analytics, insurance workflows, and regulated health commerce.

Defensibility:

- Pharmacy network.
- Verified inventory data.
- Institutional partnerships.
- Insurance and health-system integrations.
- Medication demand data.
- Compliance-first operating model.
