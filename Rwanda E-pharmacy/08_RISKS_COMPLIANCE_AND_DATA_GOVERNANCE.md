# Risks, Compliance, and Data Governance

## Regulatory Risks

Medicine sale, online pharmacy practice, and delivery require regulatory alignment. Rwanda FDA regulations indicate online pharmacy transactions need verifiable audit trails and delivery/transportation should be handled under responsible professional oversight.

Controls:

- Rwanda FDA consultation before live medicine dispensing.
- Online pharmacy compliance checklist.
- Pharmacy verification process.
- Delivery eligibility rules.
- Audit trail for all medicine transactions.

## Patient Safety Risks

Risks:

- Wrong medicine.
- Wrong dosage.
- Stale stock.
- Unsafe delivery.
- Misleading insurance information.

Controls:

- Pharmacist confirmation.
- Prescription validation.
- Stock freshness timestamps.
- Restricted medicine rules.
- Coverage disclaimers and confirmation.

## Data Privacy Risks

Risks:

- Prescription image leak.
- Patient medicine history exposed.
- Location data misuse.
- Unauthorized institutional access.

Controls:

- Consent.
- Encryption.
- Signed URLs.
- Role-based access.
- Audit logs.
- Data minimization.
- Aggregated analytics.

## Business Risks

Risks:

- Pharmacies do not update stock.
- Patients do not trust results.
- Insurers are slow to integrate.
- Delivery is operationally difficult.

Controls:

- Stock freshness score.
- Pharmacy incentives.
- Manual support during pilot.
- Start with simple insurance indication.
- Limit delivery to safe eligible medicines first.

## Data Governance Model

Data categories:

- Personal data.
- Health data.
- Prescription data.
- Pharmacy operational data.
- Analytics data.

Governance rules:

- Patient-level data stays protected.
- Institutional analytics is aggregated.
- Data exports require approval.
- Access logs are reviewed.
- Data retention rules are defined.

## Compliance Checklist

Before public launch:

- Rwanda FDA regulatory review.
- Privacy policy.
- Terms of service.
- Prescription consent.
- Location consent.
- Pharmacy verification SOP.
- Delivery SOP.
- Data sharing agreement templates.
- Incident response plan.
