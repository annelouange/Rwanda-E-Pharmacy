## Due Diligence Questions

Regulation:

- What exact approval is required to operate as or support an online pharmacy?
- Can the platform list stock from licensed pharmacies without selling directly?
- Which medicines are eligible for delivery?
- What pharmacist oversight is required?

Insurance:

- How can Mutuelle de Sante, RSSB/RAMA, MMI, and private insurers share coverage rules?
- What data can insurers receive?

Data:

- What patient consent language is required?
- How long can prescription images be stored?
- What data can be used for research and analytics?

Market:

- How many pharmacies are willing to update stock daily?
- What subscription price can pharmacies afford?
- What patient acquisition channel is cheapest?
